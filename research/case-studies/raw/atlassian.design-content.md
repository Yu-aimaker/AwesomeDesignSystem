# Content

Use our content foundations to ensure clear, concise, and consistent language throughout our apps.

![Banner for Content page](https://atlassian.design/assets/903495bc915c/static/hero.light-dcdd98626cdb06975fd2f6941d36e19cb93649a7237b9310d6dbd15d30f638da.png)

## Content guidelines

Content is at the heart of quality interfaces and apps. Use these guidelines to help you write clear and consistent UI content.

[**Inclusive language** Learn how to create inclusive content and app experiences using inclusive design principles and techniques.](https://atlassian.design/foundations/content/inclusive-writing) [**Style, grammar, and punctuation** Follow these writing conventions to make your apps and experiences clear, consistent, and localizable.](https://atlassian.design/foundations/content/language-and-grammar) [**Voice and tone** Create UI and app content that aligns with Atlassian’s voice and tone.](https://atlassian.design/foundations/content/voice-tone) [**Date and time** Communicate date and time clearly and consistently to align to wherever our customers are located.](https://atlassian.design/foundations/content/date-time) [**Designing messages** Learn how to write and format success and error messages and other types of alerts.](https://atlassian.design/foundations/content/designing-messages)
