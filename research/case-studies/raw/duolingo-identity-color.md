Our core colors are taken directly from our mascot, Duo. Here’s the common hierarchy in which they’re used:

1. Feather Green is the core color of our brand. This is the hue that the world associates with Duolingo. When in doubt, lean in to green!

2. Mask Green is our secondary green color upon which Duo can be placed.

3. Eel is commonly used for typography.

4. Snow is our primary background color.
