# Evidence & Freshness Governance

## Rules
- Canon requires reference IDs and verification notes.
- Social posts enter `content/signals` only.
- Automation labels stale sources; humans promote doctrine.
- Fetch failures ≠ content changes.

## Promotion maturity

Doctrine advances through explicit `researched → synthesized → specified → implemented → tested → adopted → measured` gates. A live source or written recommendation is not an implementation. Promotion records a bounded user need, primary evidence, comparison for high-impact rules, cross-cutting risk review, anti-imitation decision, owner, verification method, and recheck date.

Exceptions and overrides require an owner, rationale, tests, and expiry. Emerging signals remain quarantined until corroborated; adoption and outcome are measured after release.
