# Voice & Tone Matrix

Synthesized from Duolingo writing system structure + Carbon/Polaris/Atlassian content patterns (transferable; not a copy of any brand voice).

## Voice vs tone

- **Voice** = stable personality (always true).
- **Tone** = situational volume of that voice (error vs celebration).

## Required voice qualities template

Define 3–5 qualities as pairs:

| Quality | Means | Does not mean |
|---|---|---|
| Clear | concrete next step | cold or bureaucratic |
| Encouraging | progress is possible | toxic positivity |
| Playful | human warmth | unprofessional jokes in failure |
| Direct | short sentences | rude |

## Tone matrix (minimum contexts)

| Context | Energy | Formality | Humor | Length | Must include |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Onboarding | high | medium | light ok | short | why + first action |
| Empty state | medium | medium | light ok | short | next action CTA |
| Success | medium-high | low | ok | short | what changed |
| Error | low | medium | **none** | short | cause + recovery |
| Destructive confirm | low | higher | **none** | medium | irreversible impact |
| Marketing | high | low-medium | brand-specific | varies | promise without hype claims |
| Legal/security | low | high | none | precise | accurate terms |

## Character-speaking rules (if product has a mascot/persona)

- Declare **who may speak** (system vs character).
- Character may **not** deliver legal, billing, security, or health-critical messages.
- Character has a **glossary of forbidden claims**.
- Emotional range is bounded (e.g. sad ≠ cruel; excited ≠ chaotic).

## AI copy guardrails

- Never invent product features or statistics.
- Prefer canonical glossary terms.
- Errors: problem → impact → action.
- Avoid shame language ("You failed").

## Worked example structure (from Duolingo public writing system — do not copy their voice)

Duolingo publishes **four stable voice qualities** and shows each as **Means / Not** with channel-specific examples (push notifications vs in-product). Transfer the *structure*:

1. Name 4 qualities
2. For each: brief definition + "means" bullets + "not" bullets
3. Show the same quality in multiple channels
4. Keep tone separate (reads the room) while voice stays constant

Empathy rule from their tone page (transferable): when content touches hardship, **lower energy and exclamation**; when celebrating achievement, energy can rise. Success = short celebration; failure = short support without shame.

Character page pattern (Duo): publish **is / isn't** trait lists and channel-specific do/don't for notifications vs lessons. Characters may be more intense than brand voice but must not become threatening.
